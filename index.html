About The Akan Naming System

The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living in the southern regions of present-day Ghana, formerly known as the Gold Coast. While most Akans settled in Ghana, they also make up a plurality of the ethnic population in the Ivory Coast. Akan people have a first, middle, and last name or surname.
